# 📝 TOC Notemaker

**TOC Notemaker** is a sleek, professional web tool designed for Teachers On Call (TOCs) and Substitute Teachers to create beautiful, structured, and easy-to-read lesson reports. No more messy handwritten notes—generate a polished PDF that your colleagues will love.

## ✨ Features

- **🚀 Professional Reports in Seconds:** Create clean, printable lesson plans instantly.
- **📅 Automatic Schedules:** Supports regular and collaboration (Wednesday) schedules—no more calculating block times.
- **📸 Add Photos:** Easily attach photos of student work or classroom notes with custom captions.
- **🎨 Real-Time Preview:** See exactly what your report looks like while you write.
- **📱 Works on Your Phone:** Perfect for writing your notes during your lunch break or after school.
- **⚖️ Flexible Layout:** Adjust the "stretch" and "squish" of your notes to fit everything on one page.
- **🌓 Modern Design:** A beautiful, easy-on-the-eyes interface that works in any browser.

---

## 🚀 How to Get Started

TOC Notemaker is a **simple web tool** that runs directly in your browser. There is absolutely nothing to install!

1.  **Open the Tool:** Just click the link for the website (or open the `index.html` file on your computer).
2.  **No Account Needed:** You don't need to sign up or log in. Your data stays private on your own device.
3.  **Works Anywhere:** Use it on your laptop, iPad, or smartphone.

---

## 🛠 How to Create Your Report

1.  **Fill in Your Info:** Type your name and select the date and type of day (Full Day, Morning only, Afternoon only, or Multiple Days).
2.  **Add Your Notes:** For each block, type in the subject, room number, and any notes about the lesson or student behavior.
3.  **Include Photos:** Click the image icon to add photos of student work. You can label them and change their size to fit perfectly.
4.  **Check the Preview:** Look at the preview on the right side of the screen to make sure everything looks just right.
5.  **Save as PDF:** When you're finished, click **Download PDF**. You can then print it out for the teacher or email it to them directly.
